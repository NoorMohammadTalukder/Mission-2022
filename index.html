Know our
Gloabal Community

Vue.js Amsterdam is the largest Vue Conference & Community Event in the World. With 2,000+ attendees flying in from 50+ Countries over 2 Full Days. The idea came back on June 22nd 2017 when part of our organisational team teamed up with ING Bank to offer a Free Meetup at which 300 people attended. It was the community who suggested we get into the conference scene. Rumors of a framework named Vue.js gaining traction was being discussed, so the idea of Vue.js Amsterdam was born. The Rest as they say is History: Our team is proud to be delivering the most awesome, epic and must attend Vue.js Conference in the World. Established in February 2018 Vue.js Amsterdam is also proudly responsible for low-cost events run in Europe to provide a community event experience and learn from Vue.js Experts Locally in Barcelona and Berlin. Working with local communities we have run Mini Conferences in Paris, Berlin and Barcelona. Vue.js Roadtrip will continue on its Journey throughout Europe.
